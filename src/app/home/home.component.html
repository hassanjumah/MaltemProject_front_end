Movies

The present application consist of :

  • List all movies : you can visualize the database content and List all the movies

  • Add a new movie : you have the possibility to add a new movie, just click on Movies list in the menu bar, then add new movie

  • Search a movie: to search a movie , you'll need to go to list of movies, then customize your search, you can search a movie by title, director, releaseDate and type

  • Delete a movie : in case you want to delete a movie all you have to do is to click on button delete after searching your movie

  • Update movie : you can modify informations about a movie by clicking on edit button after searching your movie
